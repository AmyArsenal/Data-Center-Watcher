FAQ

Why $50/month?
It's one lunch. If you make a single site-selection decision per year informed by this, the ROI is 500-2000×. Pricing below that signals we're not a serious operator's tool — which would be wrong. (If you're a journalist or academic researcher and $50 is a hard no, email us — we have a free tier for press.)
Is this a replacement for OpenStates or LegiScan?
No — OpenStates is our upstream for raw bill text. We add the classification layer (restrictive vs. protective vs. supportive), the cross-source dedup, and the weekly editorial synthesis that tells you which bills actually matter this week and why.
What about FERC filings, court rulings, and co-location orders?
Covered. CourtListener RSS feeds into the dashboard now; FERC eLibrary is on the roadmap for June. The newsletter integrates them into each week's synthesis.
How is this different from the public dashboard?
The dashboard shows what's happened. The newsletter tells you what it means and what's coming next. The Pro data layer ranks all 50 states + every active county so you can compare sites.
Can my whole siting team get access?
Yes — Team plans start at $499/month for 5 seats. Enterprise (unlimited seats, Slack webhooks, custom dossiers, quarterly analyst calls) is a separate conversation. Email us from the About page.
Is there a free tier?
Yes — the live dashboard, the weekly brief's first section, and the sample issue are free. Full newsletter and ranked dossiers are Pro ($50/mo).
