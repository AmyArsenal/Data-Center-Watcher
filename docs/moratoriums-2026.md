# US Data Center Moratoriums — State-by-State

**Snapshot date:** 2026-04-24 · **Source:** `data/news.db` (`actions` table, `origin='research_agent'`).
Compiled by spinning out 12 parallel research agents over 50 states + federal +
courts/PUCs in April 2026. ~169 verified records added; ~46 explicitly mention a
moratorium / ban / construction freeze. Refresh cadence: weekly via the playbook
in [research-playbook.md](research-playbook.md).

## 🟢 Enacted moratoriums (in force as of April 2026)

### Statewide
- **Maine — LD 307** (passed Apr 2026, vetoed by Gov. Mills Apr 18 → on track to become law via legislative inaction). First US statewide moratorium on data centers ≥20 MW; freezes permitting until Nov 1, 2027; creates 13-member Data Center Coordination Council. House 79–62, Senate 21–13 — both short of 2/3 override.

### County-level
- **DeKalb County, GA** — Unanimous extension Dec 16, 2025 through Jun 23, 2026. Public health + environmental rationale.
- **Orange County, NC** — 6–0 one-year moratorium covering large data centers, AI training, and crypto mining. Limited siting noted (water).
- **Saline County, KS** — 4–0, three-year moratorium in unincorporated areas; bundles data centers with nuclear and hydrogen facilities.
- **Harvey County, KS** — Unanimous, in unincorporated areas through end of 2028.
- **Sedgwick County, KS** — 90-day pause extended through Jun 11, 2026; state lawmaker pushing for 3-year extension.
- **Champaign County, IL** — One-year moratorium on facilities ≥10,000 sq ft of processing space.
- **Manitowoc County, WI** — Planning + Parks Commission approved one-year moratorium (advancing to County Board). Triggered by Cloverleaf approaching Mishicot farmers.

### Cities / townships
- **Bangor, ME** — 9–0, 180-day moratorium, fast-tracked.
- **Lowell, MA** — Unanimous one-year moratorium — first in Massachusetts. Triggered by Markley Group expansion in EJ community.
- **Monterey Park, CA** — Extended through Jan 21, 2027 + ballot measure (Jun 2, 2026) for permanent ban + three nuisance ordinances.
- **Oakley, CA** — 45-day urgency moratorium, first in Bay Area; extendable to 2 yrs.
- **Pemberton Township, NJ** — Ordinance 10-2026 — first NJ municipality to ban data centers outright (Feb 20, 2026).
- **Phillipsburg, NJ** — Ordinance 2026-08 — bans data centers in all zoning districts.
- **Ravenna, OH** — 12-month moratorium following ~100-resident packed council meeting. Kent + Shalersville passed similar.
- **Tiffin, OH** — 6–0, 12-month moratorium; Findlay passed same day.
- **Oklahoma City, OK** — Unanimous moratorium through Dec 31, 2026 (two pending cases exempted).
- **Rosemount, MN** — Unanimous one-year moratorium (Meta build already underway is exempt).

## 🔴 Defeated / vetoed moratoriums

- **Maine — LD 307** — Vetoed by Gov. Mills (citing $550M Sentinel project at Androscoggin Mill). Override votes short. *(May still take effect — under Maine constitution, bills become law without signature if legislature is still in session; track outcome.)*
- **Calvert County, MD** — 2–3 vote in Apr 2026 against pursuing 24-month moratorium. Board pivoting to text amendments.
- **Hood County, TX** — 3–2 against temporary moratorium after AG Paxton letter (per Sen. Bettencourt) argued counties lack constitutional authority. Second attempt also failed late Feb.

## Adjacent restrictive actions (not strict moratoriums)

- **Alabama HB 399** — Caps tax abatements at 20 years (down from 30), effective Jan 1, 2027. Earnback to 30 yrs requires binding "qualified local investment" agreements.
- **Oregon HB 4084** — Amended to impose one-year moratorium on Standard Enterprise Zone tax subsidies for data centers.
- **Pennsylvania Capitol rally (Apr 22, 2026)** — ~150 across 15 advocacy groups + labor + community, calling for statewide moratorium.

## How to keep this current

This file is a quarterly snapshot. The live version is always in `data/news.db`:

```sql
SELECT state, jurisdiction, status, community_outcome, summary
FROM actions
WHERE action_type LIKE '%moratorium%' OR summary LIKE '%moratorium%'
ORDER BY state, last_updated DESC;
```

Static API mirror: `data/api/v1/actions/issue/zoning.json` + per-state files at
`data/api/v1/states/{XX}.json`. Refresh via the weekly playbook in
[research-playbook.md](research-playbook.md).
